About

Hello, my name is Jonathan Pelletier, and I am a remotely working Cloud Solutions Architect based in Montreal, Canada.

My primary focus is working with many different people from various business fields and realizing their visions by using the AWS Public Cloud platform as a primary tool.

This blog is about sharing what I learn and do on AWS.

The day I got started

I remember the moment distinctively when I connected the dots about AWS and realized what it would do to my daily work. I recall pondering: everything that I do from now on can sit on the same infrastructure that everyone, but the people working for a large company, can only dream off. This changes … well, everything.

Just like that, a picture of what AWS had to offer was burned into my mind. I knew I had to begin working with the platform … now. I finished my cappuccino in one gulp, and the jolt of caffeine married itself nicely with my newfound excitement. This happened circa 2012, and I have been working with AWS ever since.

Fast forward today, I am still amazed at the materializing transformation that cloud computing is enabling. Every day, I feel lucky to be part of it.
